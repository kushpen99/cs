Privacy Policy

Android Studio Code Extractor

Last updated: 8 May 2025

1. Who we are

Android Studio Code Extractor (“the App”) is a client-side web tool hosted on Vercel at https://kushpen-cs.vercel.app.
Contact: lahav.kushpen@gmail.com

2. What information we collect

Category Details When collected
Google Account basic profile Your Google OAuth identifier, name, and email address. Only after you grant consent via the Google sign-in pop-up.
OAuth access tokens Short-lived tokens for the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents scope (and drive.file if enabled). Generated by Google Identity Services after consent.
Code files you select File names and contents (e.g., .java, .xml). When you choose files/folders in the browser UI.

No server-side storage. All data stays in your browser; nothing is uploaded or logged to our servers or Vercel.

3. How we use the information

UseLegal basis
Insert your selected code into the Google Doc you choose or a new Doc created by the App. Performance of a service you request (GDPR Art. 6 (1)(b)).
Show your Google profile name in the UI (optional). Your consent provided during Google OAuth.

The App does not use your data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data or code content to any third parties. Google receives your OAuth consent and issues tokens under Google’s own Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

DataRetention policy
OAuth tokens & profile info Stored only in browser memory for the current session; deleted when you close the tab or revoke access.
Code content Held only in browser memory; disappears when you leave or reload the page.

6. Security

  • All API calls use HTTPS.
  • The site is served via Vercel’s CDN with automatic TLS.
  • No backend database—reducing server-side breach surface.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, rectify, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, email us at lahav.kushpen@gmail.com. You can also revoke the App's access via Google Account ▸ Security ▸ Third-party apps.
